WHY?

  • Whether you want to test your business idea or become a thought leader in your field, a Wordpress site can easily be set up to achieve these goals.
    With the great themes and plugins available, you can even run your business with Wordpress. And although Wordpress is relatively simple compared to learning to build your own site, there are still some tricky parts to get it right.
    That's where this workshop can help.

WHAT?

  • What is Wordpress good for?.
  • Know your way around the system.
  • Installing Themes.
  • Finding great plugins to give your site extra functionality.
  • Search engine optimisation.
  • Customising your site.
  • Using a Domain Name
  • Google Analytics to see who is using your site
  • Google Webmaster tool for advanced tools & diagnostics
