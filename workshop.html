Web design July 3
Matt Werth, Teacher
Read reviews Book Now

WHY?

  • Anyone in business either has a website - or needs a website. Knowing the basics of designing for the web helps you understand what is possible and can save you money. Perhaps you want to create a stylish web presence for yourself to showcase your achievements or a portfolio of your work. Sure, there are a lot of web applications out there that will help you do this but if you want to individualise it with your own style, you need to know HTML and CSS. HTML and CSS are the building blocks of the web and a great entry point into learning more about creating technology.

WHAT?

  • The past, present and future of the web.
  • The plumbing of the internet (how it works).
  • Planning a website and the design process.
  • Using wireframes and other design tools.
  • HTML, CSS and modern, time-saving frameworks.
  • Bootstrap v3.0 and its ease of integration.
  • Web hosting and publishing your site to the web.
  • Getting a domain name.
  • How to optimise your site for search ranking.
  • Analytics - see who is using your site.

HOW?

  • This hands on workshop is interesting, fun, and you'll get to be creative. Learn about the great free tools and heaps of tips and tricks.
    The teachers are passionate, smart and have been building websites for years.Small groups of less than 12. Teachers who have been building websites for years. An understanding of websites in simple terms. Meet some great like minded people.
    You will have fun!

WHERE?

  • Days: Saturday & Sunday
  • Times: 10am - 4pm.

  • What to bring:
  • Your own laptop, lunch money.

  • Small groups of less than 12.
  • An understanding of websites in simple terms.
  • ... You will have fun!
